ADVERTISEMENT
IN BLOGS AND VLOGS

#support

As donors of advertising, a blog exchange of bloggers is used that allows us to assign tasks and filtering out the most suitable bloggers. In addition to ordinary bloggers, we allocate youtube channels with the most potentially interested viewers and place advertisements there.

Running advertising on blogs is the stage when the target audience of the project is already fully defined - that is, after some work of contextual advertising in order to reveal those or other parameters of the most interested users.

Advertising in blogs and media advertising (youtube) allows you to embrace the blogger's audience. As a rule, in the transfer to the budget this is much less money than in the case of contextual advertising.

Separately, we want to put promotion through writing reviews and articles - when a competent blogger in this field will write an advertising review of a technical nature, emphasizing all the advantages of the product. This allows people who are following the updates of the blog to be aware of all the things that interest them in your product.
