Support

#Support

There is an opinion that the created site does not require any maintenance and support should not be paid attention. However, even a high-quality resource that is notable for its design and convenient management system, without professional support, will at best work half a power, and at worst not even reimburse the cost of its creation. Therefore, wishing to save on information and technical support, site owners, risk on the contrary to misplaced their money by receiving a low-profit or loss-making site.

This situation occurs because without support, the website, like any complicated mechanism, eventually loses its effectiveness and is susceptible to malfunctions, and this in itself is unpleasant and damaging to the business.

TECHNICAL AND INFORMATION SUPPORT

INFORMATION SUPPORT OF THE SITE

The site without actual updating of content is of little interest and ineffective. Therefore it is very important to maintain the relevance of information by updating the text and graphic components of the site. We are ready to take on this task for ourselves. Our experts create and place news and articles, will fill the catalog of the online store and empty sections of the site, adapt and add images, videos, banners.

TECHNICAL SUPPORT OF THE SITE

A website is a complex mechanism, assembled from many elements. There is always a possibility that a site may stop working or be hacked. That is why in order to prevent and timely respond to such threats, complete control over the operation of the site by a specialist performing technical support is necessary. It includes the following items:

DIAGNOSIS AND TREATMENT OF VIRUSES

Any site can be infected with a virus. This can lead to blocking of the site operation, leakage and loss of important information. Our specialists will check the site for possible vulnerabilities and conduct the necessary treatment.

HOSTING AND DOMAIN MONITORING

The loss of a domain name and hosting due to the expiration of the registration period can lead to sad consequences. Your address can be picked up, and the site files are lost. We will see to it that this does not happen.

OPERATIONAL REMEDY OF OPERATING FAULTS

A number of reasons, from virus infection to hacker attacks, can lead to instability of the site. Our task is to react to this as quickly as possible and to eliminate the problem.

BACKUP

This is the best way to protect yourself from losing the site completely or information separately. We copy the site together with the database and store on demand.

WORKING WITH CMS

We are able to support sites with all the major CMSs currently used in web development.

HOW DO WE DO IT?

STEP 1 - CONCLUSION OF THE CONTRACT

The contract is sent to the customer for an email. With the consent of the parties, the contract is completed and signed. The original contract is sent by courier or sent by post. To accelerate the process on our side, a scan-copy of the contract with seals can be sent.

STEP 2 - PAYMENT

The service provides 100% prepayment. Formed an account for the amount specified in the contract and sent to the customer for email as a scan copy. The original of the invoice is sent by post. The work is done strictly after confirmation of payment. Scan-copy of the payment order is also a confirmation of payment.

STEP 3 - ORGANIZATION OF ACCESS

To perform their duties within the framework of the contract, the performer needs full access to the resource. The customer needs to send the access to the FTP / SSH, MYSQL and the site management system to the executor if there is one. Access refers to a pair of username / password.

STEP 4 - delivery results

Upon completion of work by the contractor, the customer checks the results for compliance and, if necessary, makes adjustments. If the customer is satisfied, the contractor sends by e-mail the signed original of the act of the executed works. The work is thus considered to be completed.
